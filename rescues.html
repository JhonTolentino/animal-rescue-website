Pedro

I am a lovely, sweet, friendly boy who loves lots of fuss and cuddles! I have great recall in our field and love to chase tennis balls and bring them back to your feet to throw again! I also walk very nicely on the lead but can pull if I see another dog or a cat. Since coming back to the rescue, I have now been muzzle trained. I would like my new owner to take me to training classes to increase my confidence and give me lots of stimulation, and also help me to socialise with other dogs. I need an active home with lots of daily exercise because I am an energetic dog, though I do also like to have a nice relaxing snooze in my bed or cuddles on the sofa.

Simba

I am a very special boy looking for a long term foster home. I have been at the rescue for 3 years now patiently waiting for my forever home and now although I am given lots of love and attention I am losing my sparkle and desperately need a loving home of my own. I suffer from a few health problems which is why I am looking for a foster home. I suffer from a heart murmur, irritable bowel and I am prone to stress induced cystitis after suffering from a bladder blockage a couple of years ago. I am on a sensitive prescription diet which I will need to stay on for life but I am not on any other medication and none of these conditions have affected my cheeky and loveable nature! I am very loving with people I know and love to sit on your lap and have a cuddle. Please contact my carers if you would like to know more about me.
